HOW TO PLAY
A license plate number will be generated containing a three letter sequence.
You will have sixty seconds to guess as many words that relate to the sequence in the following way:
The first letter of the sequence is the first letter of the word.
The second letter of the sequence is somewhere in the middle of the word.
The third letter of the sequence is the last letter of the word.
